    • netlink attribute for tcp encap on xfrm states: XFRMA_ENCAP (like for udp)
    • IKETCP prefix: sent by userspace before enabling TCP_ULP (connect() side), received by userspace (accept() socket) before enabling TCP_ULP
    • non-ESP marker: will be passed to/from userspace (contrary to what the slides say). consistent with UDP encap behavior. kernel adds/strips the 2-byte length prefix to each IKE message.
